Portfolio

Kobold is the Colorado School of Mines student team competing in the 2019 NASA Robotic Mining Competition. Leveraging a combined half-century of engineering, robotics, autonomy, space resources, and mining/petroleum experience, Team Kobold is building a fully autonomous robotic mining system for use on the Moon.
